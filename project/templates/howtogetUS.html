How to Get to Us


Follow these steps to reach our location and access valuable immigration information:




Win a green card

Obtain a green card to reunite with a spouse, parents, children, or other relatives who are already U.S. citizens or permanent residents.Invest at least $1 million in the U.S. economy.or other reasons


Get a job

Receive a job offer from an employer in the U.S. who meets certain criteria.


Go to study or be unique

Obtain an invitation from an educational institution in the United States. Have special honors or talents that can benefit the United States.


Request a shelter

Show evidence that you are being persecuted in your country for political, religious or other reasons.


Get married

Marry a U.S. citizen


Settle illegally

Crossing the U.S. border without authorization or staying in the U.S. beyond the expiration date of your visa.
